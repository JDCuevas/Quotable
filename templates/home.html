About Quotable

Quotable is a website designed solely for the purpose of keeping track of your favorite quotes! You'll be able to look up quotes by famous authors, historical figures, actors, your parents (... you get the idea), and if you find some you love, you can add them to your collection for quick and easy look ups in the future. Quotable also allows you to share your wisdom by allowing you to write your own and share with friends!

So what are you waiting for? Hurry up and join us at Quotable!
